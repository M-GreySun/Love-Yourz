Me Described In Food and Drink

I don't really like telling others about myself. I foolishy believe that people can figure out what I need and want just by observing me. To be honest, I only ever spill my thoughts and feelings when I'm shaken or distrubed. Under the right circumstances I completely explode.
I can be be bitter just like lemons and limes. I'm also petty, I don't forget, and I rarely forgive. These are things that I acknowledge about myself and I'm not afraid to admit that. It's just part of who I am.
Typically described as plain, but it can also range from cloudy to clear. Water can also take on many forms. If fact water takes on the shape of it's container. Sometimes I fell as if I go above and beyond to please other. So much to the point I'm not always sure who I trully am.
I'm a nut. No question about that so of course this list needed at least one nut. Coconuts and pineapples got this tough outer shell that can be a pain to work through. However once you get past that you're rewarded with a a delicious inside. Once you get pass my armor I'm delicate in more ways than one.
What can I say, Shrek said it best, "Onions have layers". I'm a mystery and conundrum to even myself at times. To put it simply I'm complicated with lots of layers just like an onion.
Before you say I'm being too harsh on myself. I already know. You guys have been telling me that for years. I added some quotes from you guys just to show you guys that you're not talking to a brick wall.
